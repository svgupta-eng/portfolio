Sana Gupta

My name is Sana Gupta, I’m currently a Junior at UC San Diego, majoring in Data Science. I started off studying bioinformatics at UC Santa Cruz, but after an Applied Linear Algebra course, I fell in love with the math. I realized rather than just solving equations on paper, I wanted to use those concepts to build systems that could learn from data. For the past year, I’ve been working heavily with Large Language Models. As an AI Data Quality Analyst for a FAANG company, I designed protocols to benchmark GenAI models and reduce hallucinations. Before that, I built ETL pipelines to process unstructured text data for the Department of Statistics.
